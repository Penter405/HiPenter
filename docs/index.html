Why HiPenter?

Built for people, not platforms.

🔐

You Own Your Data

Every post is saved in a Google Sheet on your Drive. Export it, delete it, keep it forever — it's yours.

🌍

Decentralized Feed

No central database owns your content. The feed aggregates posts from individual sheets — truly distributed.

✨

15 Words Max

Brevity is the soul of wit. Say more with less. Every word matters when you only get fifteen.

🚫

No Ads. No Tracking.

Zero analytics, zero trackers, zero ads. The app doesn't even have a backend database.

⚡

Blazing Fast

Edge-cached feed via Vercel proxy. Offline-first with local cache. Loads instantly every time.

🔓

Open Source

Every line of code is public on GitHub. Fork it, modify it, host your own instance.

Architecture

Simple by design.

1

Sign in with Google

Uses the modern Credential Manager API. We only need permission to create a Sheet in your Drive.

2

Post your thoughts

Your post is written to your personal Sheet first, then a copy goes to the shared feed via a Vercel proxy.

3

Read the global feed

The feed is aggregated from everyone's posts, cached at the edge for speed. No quota worries.

4

Delete anytime

Delete from the app, or delete your entire Sheet. You have full control, always.

100% Open Source

Trust, but verify.

HiPenter is fully open-source under the MIT license. The Android app, the Vercel proxy, the Apps Script backend — everything is on GitHub. You can audit every line that touches your data.
